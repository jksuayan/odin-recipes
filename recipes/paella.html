Paella

Description

A very traditional paella, garnished with chorizo, chicken, peas, squid, mussels, and shrimp. Chorizo is a sausage spiced with garlic and chili powder; remove casing before cooking. A paella pan is recommended.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 ounces Spanish chorizo (cured spiced pork sausage), casing discarded and sausage cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 (12 ounce) package uncooked Arborio rice
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 squid, cleaned and cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound mussels, cleaned and debearded
  • ¼ cup chopped Italian flat leaf parsley
  • 8 slices lemon, for garnish

Steps

A.

  1. Heat olive oil in paella pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic and pepper; cook and stir for a few minutes.
  3. Add chorizo sausage, diced chicken, and rice; cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in 3 1/2 cups stock, wine, thyme leaves, and saffron.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring to the boil, and simmer for 15 minutes; stir occasionally.

B.

  1. Taste the rice, and check to see if it is cooked.
  2. If the rice is uncooked, stir in 1/2 cup more stock.
  3. Continue cooking, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in additional stock if necessary: use up to 2 cups additional stock, 5 cups total.
  5. Cook until rice is done.

C.

  1. Stir in squid, tomatoes, and peas. Cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Arrange prawns and mussels on top.
  3. Cover with foil, and leave for 3 to 5 minutes.

D.

  1. Remove the foil, and scatter parsley over the food.
  2. Serve in paella pan, garnished with lemon wedges.

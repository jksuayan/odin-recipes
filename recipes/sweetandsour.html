Sweet and Sour Chicken

Description

Make restaurant-worthy sweet and sour chicken in the comfort of your own home with this top-rated recipe.

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • water
  • canned pineapple juice
  • white sugar
  • white vinegar
  • orange food coloring

For the batter:

  • cornstarch
  • self-rising flour
  • vegetable oil
  • egg
  • salt
  • white pepepr

Others:

  • chicken: cut eight boneless, skinless chicken breast halves into 1-inch cubes
  • pineapple chunks
  • two green bell peppers (cut into 1-inch pieces)

Steps

  1. Boil 1 Â½ cups of water with pineapple juice, sugar, vinegar, and orange food coloring.
  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickened.
  3. Make the batter, then stir in the chicken pieces until well-coated.
  4. Fry the chicken.
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a platter.
  6. Pour the sauce over the top.
